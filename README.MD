## 🙋‍♂️ About Me

I am a Senior AI & ML Systems Architect with over 8 years of experience designing and deploying secure, scalable AI platforms in enterprise environments. My expertise lies in architecting distributed machine learning systems, Kubernetes-native orchestration, multi-cloud platforms, and Generative AI solutions—including Large Language Models (LLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and complex agentic workflows. 

Throughout my career, I have successfully led end-to-end ML pipeline development, spearheaded microservices transformations, and driven data processing efficiency across top organizations. I maintain a strong focus on MLOps best practices, IAM-based access control, system auditability, and operational resilience to deliver high-impact engineering solutions.

Currently based in Bengaluru, I am actively exploring the latest advancements in AI technologies, building next-generation agentic side projects, and actively contributing to the open-source community.
